Gili Privacy Policy Last updated: July 2026 Gili is a private, invite-based messaging app for one-to-one conversations and selective sharing. This policy explains what information Gili collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you. Important SMS privacy statement: Gili uses SMS only for account verification and sign-in codes requested by you. Gili does not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers or SMS consent with third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Information We Collect Gili may collect: - Account login information, such as your phone number and supported sign-in providers. - Verification information needed to send and confirm one-time sign-in codes. - App metadata needed to operate the service, such as device identifiers, connection records, timestamps, and invite pass status. - Content you choose to create or share in Gili, such as messages, profile photos, contact identities, voice notes, and media. How We Use Information Gili uses information: - To create and protect your account. - To send one-time verification codes when you request phone sign-in. - To create invite passes, connect chats, route messages, and manage share controls. - To prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, and keep the service reliable. SMS Verification When you enter your phone number and request a private code, Gili asks its SMS provider to send a one-time verification code. Message frequency depends on how often you request a code. Message and data rates may apply. Gili does not send marketing text messages. Service Providers Gili may use trusted service providers, including Supabase for authentication and backend services and Twilio for SMS delivery. These providers process information only as needed to operate Gili and provide the requested services. Security And Storage Gili is designed to minimize what the server can see. Chat messages, voice notes, and media are intended to be encrypted before storage or transit through Gili services. Some account and routing metadata, such as login identifiers, device state, invite status, timestamps, and delivery state, may be stored to operate the app. Sharing Choices Gili is built around explicit sharing. You choose which profile or contact items a connection can see. If you remove or stop sharing an item, Gili will stop showing that item in the app, but another person may already have copied, saved, or screenshotted information they previously saw. Retention Gili keeps information only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with applicable obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and maintain security. Local chat history may remain on your device unless you delete it or clear the app data. Your Choices - You can choose what to share in each connection. - You can request a new verification code only when you need to sign in. - You can opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP where supported. Phone sign-in may not work while SMS is blocked. - You can request account deletion from inside the app when the feature is available.
